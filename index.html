About Us

SIDS Vidya Sansthan is a premier educational institution in Samastipur, Bihar. We offer quality education from Nursery to Class IX with a focus on fostering curiosity and excellence. Our curriculum follows the CBSE pattern and integrates modern teaching methodologies.

A Unit of Shree Rash Bihari Mission Trust: Shree Rash Bihari Mission is a non-profit organisation established in 2004. It works primarily in the domains of Education, Health, Employment, Art & Culture, Governance, Gender, Child & Youth Development, Technology, and Specially Abled. Its primary office is in Bhagalpur, Bihar.
